# Roadmap — PlaySight AI

Post-MVP features in rough order.

---

## v0.2 — Auth & persistence

- User accounts (email/password or OAuth)
- My videos / My results
- Link jobs and assets to user

---

## v0.3 — Mistake detection

- Consume tracks + events in new analytics module
- Rule-based or model: over-committing, ball-watching, spacing errors, slow recovery
- Natural-language feedback with timestamp (“In the 63rd minute, you drifted too narrow as a fullback.”)

---

## v0.4 — Tactical templates & scorecard

- Position-specific “ideal” heatmap templates
- Compare player heatmap to template; spacing/positioning score
- Performance scorecard: position rating, match-to-match progression

---

## v0.5 — Multi-sport

- Basketball, football, lacrosse, etc.
- Sport-specific detection and event definitions

---

## v1.0 — Monetization & growth

- B2C subscription ($15–30/mo)
- Team / club plans (B2B)
- Recruiting add-on: college exposure, verified analytics

---

## Later

- AI Coach Mode, training plan generator
- Wearables (heart rate, sprint load)
- Real-time sideline feedback
- Talent discovery, club dashboards, data marketplace
